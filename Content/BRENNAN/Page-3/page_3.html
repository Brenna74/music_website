Current state of pop music

Recently Pop has made a significant comeback after briefly falling behing hip-hop due to dance-pop, electropop and synthpop.

Pop is currently enjoying chart domination.

After being unpopular for most of the time since the turn of the millenium teen pop boy bands have gained widespread popularity thanks to acts like The Wanted and One Direction enjoying huge success.

Current popular pop artists include, Ed Sheeran, Ellie Goulding, Katy Perry, Lady Gaga, and Justin Bieber.
